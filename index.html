About Meshy

◐ Generate Mesh Gradients - Intro

Meshy is design tool to generate mesh gradients, with a given color palette.
Each generated gradient is unique to id & color hex codes, which behaves as a seed to recreate the gradients. The Column on the right has few presets of curated mesh gradients.
A Palette of only four colors is used to create gradient where each color is associated with differnt amount of coverage area, (i.e replace the colors amount of area covered by original colors)

◐ Download Mesh Gradient PNGs - Steps

1. Click on the colors to change the selected color Palette
2. Press Randomize Button to generate random its random variant untill you find the perfect gradient
3. Click on save & adjust width & height to download custom resolution PNGs.
